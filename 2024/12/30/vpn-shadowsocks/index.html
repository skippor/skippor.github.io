报错解决

1. AttributeError: /lib64/libcrypto.so.1.1: undefined symbol: EVP_CIPHER_CTX_cleanup

  • 将/usr/local/lib/python3.6/site-packages/shadowsocks/crypto/openssl.py 里的 EVP_CIPHER_CTX_cleanup 替换为 EVP_CIPHER_CTX_reset 即可
  • 或者用python3低版本
